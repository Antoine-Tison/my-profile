Rugby

I started this sport because my parents pushed me to go to the trainings and I realised that I loved this sport. I started rugby in Hong-Kong for six years and then I continued in France when I arrived in 2012. I was playing in a great team which is called Stade Francais Paris. I just changed team this year to ally my passion to my studies. I have three trainings per week and I still love this sport after sixteen years of practicing.

Hong-Kong

I used to live in Hong-Kong from my one to seven years old. I remember this period a lot even if i was really young. Hong-Kong is a little island just on the East side of China. The climate was very different from France because it was tropical. I loved this city because we could find huge towers and just next to it, an enormous tropical jungle. I think it was a chance to live over there because I could discover lots of things like new cultures or new landscapes. Today, I live in France and I love it too but I couldn't say whichone I prefer.
